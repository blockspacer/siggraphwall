SIGGRAPH Image Wall

SIGGRAPH Image Wall is an experimental project to ease navigation between research works in SIGGRAPH by providing a gallery-like website.

About this project

SIGGRAPH Image Wall initiates from one afternoon in Taipei street. I was going home by bus after submitting research works to SIGGRAPH Asia 2013. In academic computer graphics, Ke-Sen Huang's paper collection is a great portal site for this field. However, I was wondering what would happen if those tough academic research works were presented as an image gallery? What if we navigated research works through Pinterest-like way? And this is the working-on result of the idea.
